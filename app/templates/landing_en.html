What is geolocation lookup?

This application allows you to perform fast and reliable geolocation lookups for any given IP address. By entering an IP, you can retrieve useful information such as the country, region, city, latitude, longitude, timezone, and more. It is a lightweight microservice designed to be simple to use, whether through the web interface or command-line requests. Developers can easily integrate it into their projects for location-based services, analytics, or security monitoring.

Simple Geolocation Lookup API!

$ curl http://localhost:8000/geolocation/all/212.8.253.145
{"all":{"ip":"212.8.253.145","country_short":"NL", "country_long":"Netherlands (Kingdom of the)","region":"Zuid-Holland", "city":"Naaldwijk","latitude":"51.994064","longitude":"4.209895", "zipcode":"2673","timezone":"+02:00"}}
$ curl http://localhost:8000/geolocation/country/212.8.253.145
{"country_code":"NL"}
$ curl http://localhost:8000/geolocation/city/212.8.253.145
{"city_name":"Naaldwijk"}
$ curl http://localhost:8000/geolocation/coordinates/212.8.253.145
{"coordinates":"51.994064, 4.209895"}
